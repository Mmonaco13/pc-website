OUR HISTORY

Polar Cub was purchased by its current owner in December 1998. Although Polar Cub's official beginnings is unknown, the timeframe has been pinpointed to around the 1950's.

We love to hear about families that have been visiting the Polar Cub for generations and the family traditions that have been built over the years.

It is our hope that we continue to embody the values that were instilled around 70 years ago and that we continue to give every customer the best experience possible.
